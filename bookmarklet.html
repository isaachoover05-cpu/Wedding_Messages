💍 Save to Wedding

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar

  1. Show your bookmarks bar — Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + B.
  2. Drag the gold button up onto the bar. It becomes a bookmark.
  3. While browsing any shop or inspiration page, click 💍 Save to Wedding.
  4. A small window opens with the page's title, link, price and description pre-filled — pick a category, rate it, and save. The window closes itself.

Not signed in? The window will ask you to log in first, then continue straight to the save form. Works for both of you.
